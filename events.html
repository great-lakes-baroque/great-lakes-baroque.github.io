Events from our Third Season


15 June 2018: Great Lakes Baroque brewed fun with an all-Bach program

Great Lakes Baroque founders and internationally-renowned harpsichordists Jory Vinikour and Philippe LeRoy and The Great Lakes Baroque Ensemble were joined by soprano Kristin Knutson, tenor Scott Brunscheen, bass Ryan de Ryke, violinist Allison Nyquist, and flautist Leela Breithaupt in two performances of an all-Bach program including Brandenburg Concerto No. 5 (BWV 1050), Concerto for two harpsichords in C major (BWV 1061), the cantata ‘Non sa che sia dolore’ (BWV 209), and ‘Schweight stille, plaudert nicht’ (BWV 211), the effervescent ‘Coffee Cantata.’

15 June 2018
4:00 PM and 7:00 PM
Cabot Theatre at Broadway Theatre Center
158 N. Broadway
Milwaukee, Wisconsin  53202

13 October 2017: Music for the Last Queen

Acclaimed violinist Allison Edberg Nyquist and cellist Paul Dwyer joined keyboard virtuoso and Great Lakes Baroque co-founder Philippe LeRoy in Music for the Last Queen, a delightful programme featuring music by French composers active in the last quarter of the Eighteenth Century. Composers whose music will be performed include Jean-Jacques Beauvarlet-Charpentier, Armand-Louis Couperin, Jean-Baptiste Janson, Amédée Rasetti, Nicolas Séjan, and Simon Simon.

Friday, 13 October 2017, 7:30 PM [Pre-performance lecture at 6:30 PM]
North Shore Congregational Church
7330 North Santa Monica Boulevard
Fox Point, Wisconsin  53217

13 September 2015: Io vidi in terra

Presenting a programme of Italian songs and duets from the Seventeenth Century, Io vidi in terra brought internationally-renowned soloists mezzo-soprano Céline Ricci and countertenor José Lemos together with Deborah Fox on theorbo and Baroque guitar, Baroque cellist Craig Trompeter, and harpsichordist Jory Vinikour. The music of Claudio Monteverdi, Marco da Gagliano, Barbara Strozzi, Tarquinio Merula, Agostino Steffani, and other composers resounded in Great Lakes Baroque's first concert.

Sunday, 13 September 2015, 7:30 PM
St. Joseph's Convent Chapel
Milwaukee, Wisconsin  53215
