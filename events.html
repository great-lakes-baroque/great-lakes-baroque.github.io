Ticket Information


Tickets for most GLB events are available online in advance. Tickets for all events are available at the door. Prices for events may vary. Please refer to individual pricing for each event when adding tickets to your PayPal shopping cart. Always remember to retain your PayPal receipt, as this will serve as proof of purchase for admission to event(s): separate tickets will not be issued for most events.

Adult [General Admission] - $30.75 online | $30 at the door
Senior Citizens (age 65+) [General Admission] - $20.50 online | $20 at the door
Students, Veterans, Unemployed (with valid ID) [General Admission] - $5.15 online | $5 at the door

13 September 2015: Io vidi in terra

Presenting a programme of Italian songs and duets from the Seventeenth Century, Io vidi in terra brought internationally-renowned soloists mezzo-soprano Céline Ricci and countertenor José Lemos together with Deborah Fox on theorbo and Baroque guitar, Baroque cellist Craig Trompeter, and harpsichordist Jory Vinikour. The music of Claudio Monteverdi, Marco da Gagliano, Barbara Strozzi, Tarquinio Merula, Agostino Steffani, and other composers resounded in Great Lakes Baroque's first concert.

Sunday, 13 September 2015, 7:30 PM
St. Joseph's Convent Chapel
Milwaukee, WI 53215
