About Great Lakes Baroque

Founded in 2015 by internationally-acclaimed harpsichordists Jory Vinikour and Philippe LeRoy, Great Lakes Baroque, Ltd., is dedicated to bringing the extraordinary richness of Baroque music to the greater Milwaukee metropolitan area in performances featuring some of the finest musicians in the world. From Purcell to Rameau, Bach to Cavalli, join Great Lakes Baroque on artistic journeys that unite great music, great artists, and great audiences in unforgettable musical experiences.
