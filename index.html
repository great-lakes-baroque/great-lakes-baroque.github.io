28 February 2016: From Westminster to Wisconsin - If music be the food of love

Featuring songs and duets by Henry Purcell (1659 - 1695), If music be the food of love unites sopranos Chelsea Morris and Erica Schuller with the instrumental trio of David Walker (theorbo), Craig Trompeter (Baroque cello and viola da gamba), and Great Lakes Baroque founder Jory Vinikour (harpsichord) in performances of music by England's greatest composer of the Seventeenth Century.

Sunday, 28 February 2016, 5:00 PM
North Shore Congregational Church
