Jory Vinikour, Artistic Director
Support GLB, a 501(c)(3) non-profit!





Collaborating with some of the world's most accomplished musicians, internationally-acclaimed and GRAMMY®-nominated harpsichordist and conductor Jory Vinikour and Great Lakes Baroque bring extraordinary performances of Baroque music to beautiful venues in metropolitan Milwaukee and the Great Lakes region.

Rome, Paris, London, Leipzig, and the most exciting destinations in Baroque music come to Milwaukee’s doorstep. Join us!
