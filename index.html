Great Lakes Baroque presents Music for the Last Queen

Acclaimed violinist Allison Edberg Nyquist and cellist Paul Dwyer join keyboard virtuoso and Great Lakes Baroque co-founder Philippe LeRoy in Music for the Last Queen, a delightful programme featuring music by French composers active in the last quarter of the Eighteenth Century. Composers whose music will be performed include Jean-Jacques Beauvarlet-Charpentier, Armand-Louis Couperin, Jean-Baptiste Janson, Amédée Rasetti, Nicolas Séjan, and Simon Simon.

Venez vous joindre à nous et nos amis francophones à 18h30. Philippe LeRoy dira quelques mots (en français et en anglais) sur cette musique, sur les compositeurs et sur l'époque qui produisait cette musique si brillante et si légère à l'aube de la Révolution.

Come join us and our French-speaking friends at 6:30 p.m. Philippe LeRoy will say a few words (in French and in English) about the works, the composers, and the epoch which produced such brilliant and light music at the dawn of the French Revolution.
